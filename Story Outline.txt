Story Outline:

===============================
Draft 1
===============================
Part 1:
Begin at Svoryk's Castle
Go in, get mistaken for someone who will save the kingdom
Forces of Krill arrive
They are repelled, but repelling them cost heavy damage to castle

Part 2:
Someone has the bright idea of sending you to ally with warlock Malifestro over the mountains
Journey under the mountains
Once arrive, convince the warlock to help drive out Krill

Part 3:
Krill and Warlock ally together, take you captive
Each comes to you in secret to help overthrow the other
Given free access to all areas
Can help either win, or can subvert both somehow (true ending)
